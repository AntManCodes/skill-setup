Why We're Different

Wood-Fired Tradition

Our hand-built stone oven burns seasoned oak at 900°F, creating a signature blistered crust and smoky aroma that electric ovens simply cannot replicate.

Farm-Fresh Ingredients

We partner exclusively with local heritage farms for our San Marzano-style tomatoes, hand-stretched mozzarella, and seasonal heirloom toppings.

House-Made Dough

A 72-hour slow fermentation process using high-protein flour results in a light, airy, and easily digestible dough with deep flavor complexity.
